How CTF Works

Three steps from challenge to victory.

1

Find a Challenge

Browse challenges across 6 categories — web, crypto, pwn, reversing, forensics, and misc. Each challenge hides a secret string called a flag.

2

Capture the Flag

Exploit vulnerabilities, decode messages, or analyze files to find the hidden flag string. Think creatively and use your toolkit.

3

Submit & Score

Submit CTF{...} to earn points. The team with the most points at the end of the competition wins. Most CTFs are 24–72 hours.
